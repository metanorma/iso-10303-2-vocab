# Terms and Definitions

This dataset contains the terms and definitions from **Clause 3 of ISO 10303-2**, the vocabulary standard for the ISO 10303 series (STandard for the Exchange of Product model data — STEP).

## What's Included

685 terms and definitions organized into 32 thematic sections, covering the vocabulary needed to understand and work with the ISO 10303 series:

- **General terms** — fundamental concepts such as product, application protocol, application module, and data exchange
- **EXPRESS and EXPRESS-X language** — terms for the data modeling languages used to define STEP schemas
- **SysML** — terms related to the Systems Modeling Language mappings
- **Standard data access interfaces** — terms for SDAI and programmatic access to STEP data
- **XML representations** — terms for XML-based encoding of EXPRESS schemas and data
- **Conformance testing** — methodology and framework terms for testing STEP implementations
- **Geometric and topological representation** — shape modelling, boundary representation, curves, surfaces, and tolerances
- **Product structure and configuration** — assembly structure, configuration management, and product lifecycle
- **Material and engineering properties** — mechanical, thermal, and other engineering property terms
- **Visual presentation** — rendering, appearance, and display-related terms
- **Finite element analysis** — mesh, node, element, and analysis result terminology
- **Computational fluid dynamics** — mesh-based CFD terms
- **Process structure** — manufacturing process modelling vocabulary
- **And more** — covering additional domains across the ISO 10303 series

## How to Use This Dataset

Each term provides a normative definition approved by ISO/TC 184/SC 4. Terms are linked to related concepts and to the EXPRESS entity types they describe. Use the search and graph features to explore relationships between terms across domains.

## Source

These terms are extracted from the [ISO 10303 source repository](https://github.com/iso-10303/iso-10303) and maintained by ISO/TC 184/SC 4/TF 1.
